RPSLS

Rock · Paper · Scissors · Lizard · Spock

The classic hand game, extended with two extra moves. Lizard poisons Spock, Spock vaporizes Rock. Every move beats two others and loses to two others. Total balance — and a lot more fun.

Why you'll stay

Polished, deep, fast.

🌐
Real-time multiplayer

WebSocket rooms, random matchmaking, private 6-letter lobby codes, auto-reconnect on Wi-Fi hiccups, haptic feedback on every reveal.

🤖
Three AI difficulties

Easy plays into your hand. Normal is mood-weighted random. Hard reads your last 5 moves and counters your habits.

🎴
Hand-crafted variants

RPSLS canonical, Monsters (Vampire/Unicorn/Clown/Zombie/Ghost), Arsenal (Bow/Sword/Hammer/Spear/Shield). Each pair has its own verb.

🏅
Quests & daily challenge

12 trackable objectives plus a different daily match every day with a +50% XP bonus.

🌍
15 languages

EN · FR · ES · DE · IT · PT · ZH · JA · RU · AR (RTL) · KO · HI · TR · PL · NL. Use the flag toggle ↗ on this very page.

🎨
5 themes · 5 boards

Violet, Neon, Pastel, Sunset, Forest — paired with five hand-illustrated battle pads from Blackboard to Cosmos to Comics.

# Prompts — Illustrations des 8 cartes FUSIONNÉES (Constellation Pro)

> But : générer l'art des cartes nées de la Forge ⚗. **Style commun** à coller
> en tête de chaque prompt pour la cohérence avec le reste du deck.

## Style commun (préambule à répéter)

```
Digital painting, premium fantasy trading-card-game illustration, single
centered subject, dramatic volumetric lighting, rich rim light, painterly
detail, cosmic/arcane ambiance, vertical 4:5 framing, NO text, NO frame, NO UI,
no watermark. Subtle magenta-to-amber palette accent (the colour of the Forge).
High contrast, crisp focal point, slight depth-of-field blur on the background.
```

---

## 1. FRAPPE PARFAITE  (Précision + Surge — +6 ATK, le coup parfait)
```
A warrior's blade frozen at the instant of a flawless strike, a razor-thin
golden trajectory line tracing the perfect arc, sparks and a single shockwave
ring, eyes glowing with focus. Sense of unstoppable precision power. Gold + white
energy on deep indigo.
```

## 2. FORTERESSE / BASTION  (Aegis + Ancre — bouclier + ancre + provocation)
```
An impregnable arcane bastion: a towering stone guardian fused with a radiant
hexagonal energy shield and a massive anchor embedded at its base, immovable.
Defensive, monumental, taunting stance. Sapphire shield glow, weathered stone,
amber runes.
```

## 3. AVALANCHE  (Jet de Caillou ×2 — 3 dégâts à 2 créatures)
```
A cataclysmic rockslide thundering down a mountain, two massive boulders
hurtling forward toward the viewer, dust and debris, motion blur, raw kinetic
destruction. Earthy browns and slate grey with a faint amber dawn behind.
```

## 4. SOURCE VITALE  (Sève + Second Souffle — +3 créature ET +3 héros)
```
A luminous fountain of living sap and golden light erupting from an ancient
world-tree root, healing motes rising, lush vines drinking the glow. Restorative,
serene yet powerful. Emerald + warm gold, soft god-rays.
```

## 5. OMNISCIENCE  (Oracle + Coup d'œil — pioche 3 + main révélée)
```
A colossal all-seeing cosmic eye opening within a constellation, threads of
starlight revealing hidden cards/runes floating around it, third-eye awakening.
Mystical, knowing, vast. Violet + cyan nebula, gold iris.
```

## 6. COCON  (Toile Gluante + Malédiction — n'attaque pas + −2 ATK)
```
A creature fully bound and silenced inside a sickly luminescent web-cocoon,
strands cinching tight, a faint cursed purple aura draining its strength.
Claustrophobic, helpless, eerie. Lime-green web, bruised violet curse glow.
```

## 7. APOCALYPSE  (Supernova + Gravité — 4 dégâts à TOUS + 4 au héros)
```
A dying star collapsing then detonating into a galaxy-ending blast, a black-hole
core ringed by a fiery shockwave consuming everything, debris pulled and torn.
End-of-all-things scale. Searing white-orange core, crimson and deep-void edges.
```

## 8. IMPOSTEUR  (Larcin + Mascarade — vole 1 carte + lit la main)
```
A masked rogue mid-heist wearing a stolen face, peeling off one identity to
reveal another beneath, a pilfered glowing card slipping into their grasp,
sly smirk. Deceptive, elegant, dangerous. Theatrical masks, magenta + gold,
shadowy backdrop.
```
